Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business days delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be returned in original condition with all packaging.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer exclusive student discounts on many products. Create an account with your college email to access these deals.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track orders from your profile dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information.
